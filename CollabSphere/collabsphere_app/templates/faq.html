Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CollabSphere

General Questions

▼

What is CollabSphere?

+

CollabSphere is a team management platform designed for remote and hybrid teams. It helps streamline communication, manage events, track team wellbeing, monitor check-ins, and organize tasks efficiently.

Who can use CollabSphere?

+

Any team—remote, hybrid, or on-site—can use CollabSphere. It is ideal for companies, organizations, schools, and project-based teams needing structured collaboration tools.

Is CollabSphere mobile-friendly?

+

Yes. CollabSphere is designed to be responsive and accessible on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

Account & Access

▼

How do I create an account?

+

Users can sign up using their company email or personal email. After registration, they must verify their email to access full features.

Can I join multiple teams?

+

Yes. Users can join multiple teams as long as they receive an invitation from each team admin.

Events Module

▼

How do I create an event?

+

Go to the Events tab → click "Create Event" → fill in details such as title, date, time, and description → click Save.

Can team members receive event reminders?

+

Yes. Email or in-app reminders are automatically sent before the scheduled event.

Can I edit or cancel an event after posting it?

+

Yes. Event creators or admins can update or cancel events at any time.

Check-ins Module

▼

What is a check-in?

+

A check-in allows team members to log their daily work status, accomplishments, challenges, and availability.

How often should I submit check-ins?

+

Check-ins can be daily, weekly, or based on team guidelines set by the admin.

Can managers view team check-in history?

+

Yes. Managers and admins can view all submitted check-ins for performance monitoring and support.

Wellbeing Module

▼

What is the wellbeing feature used for?

+

It helps teams monitor overall mood, stress levels, and wellbeing through simple mood check surveys.

Is wellbeing data confidential?

+

Yes. Individual responses are kept private, and only summarized analytics are visible to managers.

How often can I submit my wellbeing status?

+

Users can submit once per day or as required by team guidelines.

Task & Team Management

▼

How do I assign a task to a team member?

+

Go to the Tasks tab → click Assign Task → select a member → set due date → add details → save.

Can I track task progress?

+

Yes. Task statuses (To-Do, In Progress, Completed) help track updates in real time.

Technical & Support

▼

Does CollabSphere work offline?

+

No. An internet connection is required for real-time updates and synchronization.

What browsers are supported?

+

CollabSphere supports Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari.
